Hey there, looking to get involved with your community?

Look no further.

Getting involved is good for the community, good for your mental health, and brings us all together. Here are some of our upcoming events.

Seed Starting Workshop

Learn the basics of starting seeds indoors to get a jump on the growing season. Perfect for beginners!

Kids' Garden Day

A fun-filled day for children to learn about plants, soil, and the joy of gardening through interactive activities.

Composting 101

Discover how to turn kitchen scraps into nutrient-rich compost to enrich your garden soil.

Herb Garden Planting

Join us in planting a variety of herbs and learn how to care for them throughout the season.

Garden Art for Kids

Encourage creativity as children create garden-themed art projects to take home and display.

Harvest Festival

Celebrate the season's bounty with music, food, and community activities for all ages.

It's okay if you missed an event

If you see an event that you missed and you'd like to attend, let us know. We sometimes bring back events with high demand in the future.

Missed an Event?

If you see an event you missed and would love another chance to attend, let us know! We sometimes bring back popular events by request.

Garden Planning Workshop

Learn how to design garden beds, rotate crops, and plan out a successful season from start to finish.

Kids' Pollinator Hunt

A fun, guided exploration for kids to spot bees, butterflies, and other garden helpers!

Rainwater Collection Demo

Discover sustainable ways to collect rainwater and reduce your garden’s environmental footprint.

Vertical Gardening

Learn to grow more in less space with trellises, towers, and creative vertical solutions.

Garden Yoga & Meditation

Join us for a relaxing morning of yoga among the plants — great for your body and mind.

Community Plant Swap

Bring your extra seedlings and swap them with others! A great way to diversify your garden and connect with neighbors.
